Eat In The Sky Restaurant

Eat In The Sky Restaurant has been serving up fresh and homemade pizza since 1999. Our unique focus on using locally-sourced ingredients brings a delicious and authentic taste to our pizza. Customers can choose from over 20 different pizza styles, all made to order with a real tomato sauce and cheese that will leave you wanting more. Using a grandma recipe, Eat In The Sky Restaurant has perfected their greasy and satisfying pizza that will satisfy your cravings at a low price. Come and feel the taste of pizza, made fresh just for you at Eat In The Sky Restaurant.

If you are in Midtown Manhattan anytime from 12 pm to 12 am, be sure to check out Eat in The Sky Restaurant. Located near Central Park, MOMA, Carnegie Hall, Rockefeller Center, Radio City Music Hall, and Times Square, it's the perfect spot for a delicious meal in the heart of the city. With its prime location and reputation as the best restaurant in Midtown Manhattan, you won't be disappointed.
